Safe and Relaxed Hairdressing

Hi, I am Ellie. Welcome to my hair page!

I am offering haircuts in the open space of the Granary at Horam Manor Farm in East Sussex. You can expect a great hair cut, fresh air and relaxation. Well away from the hustle and bustle of a busy salon environment.

I trained in London with the Aveda Institute & Tusk Hairdressing. I worked as a Top Stylist at Tusk and Flaxton Ptootch salon in London before moving to Sussex. I have also travelled globally for session work and weddings.

I am passionate about our natural environment and the use of natural, organic hair care and styling products. I avoid using single-use plastics.

Contact me with the Whatsapp link above for a consultation before your appointment. You can send me photos of haircuts you like, your hair in its natural state, and from when you loved it.

I am not a magician, but I do try!

COVID Safety

I have completed the Barbicide COVID 19 Certification for the Professional Beauty Industry Training. I will be compliant with current COVID 19 working practices which include the use of a face shield.

To read full risk assessment and details of my working practice click here

I am currently offering haircuts at the Granary. I have chosen this space for the open nature and to cater to people who might be anxious visiting a regular salon.

Please arrive with your hair washed to reduce contact time and because The Granary does not have a basin. If you are interested in colour, I am happy to see if we can find an alternative space for this.

Please bring your facemask to wear. I may ask you to remove this to cut your hair (around ears etc.)

I will wear a visor, and/or a facemask throughout your haircut. I may not wear this when you first arrive but will be sure to maintain a safe distance until I put it on.

  • Disposable, compostable towels for each client.
  • Hand sanitiser on entry
  • No touchpoints – no basin, no door handles, no magazines.

There is a WC next to the barn with handwashing facilities.

I will provide hand sanitiser or bring your own. I will ask you to please apply this on arrival.
