Safe and Relaxed Hairdressing

Hi! I am offering affordable, quality haircuts that will last. Working in my quiet garden you will get an hour of peace and a gimmick free service.

Contact me with the Whatsapp link above for a consultation before your appointment. You can send me photos of haircuts you like, your hair in its natural state, and from when you loved it.

I will be compliant with current COVID 19 working practices including the use of a face shield and a disposable gown. I have completed the Barbicide COVID 19 Certification for the Professional Beauty Industry Training My full risk assessment and details of my working practice can be viewed here.

My Backgound

I trained in London with the Aveda Institute and I am a specialist in colour and extensions. Before moving to Horam I worked as a senior stylist at Tusk, Camden and Flaxton Ptootch, Kentish Town. I have travelled globally for session work and weddings.

I am not a magician, but I do try!
